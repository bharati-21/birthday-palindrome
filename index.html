Check out if your Birthdate is a Palindrome

Palindrome is a word/ phrase/ sequence that reads the same backwards as forwards.

Try it out!

Enter your Birthdate to know if it is a Palindrome

Your birthdate is checked in 4 formats: mm-dd-yyyy, dd-mm-yyyy, mm-dd-yy, and yyyy-mm-dd

E.g. If your birthdate is 21st July 1999, then the app will check for:
07211999, 21071999, 072199, 19990721
